About Your Coach

Committed to Every Client, Every Day

NASM Certified Personal Trainer National Academy of Sports Medicine — the industry standard in evidence-based fitness programming.
Online & In-Person Coaching Available to clients in Springfield, VA and remotely to anyone, anywhere in the country.
Specialization: Fat Loss & Body Recomposition Structured, science-backed programming designed for adults who want lasting, measurable change.

I became a personal trainer because I believe most people aren't lacking motivation — they're lacking a system. The right plan, built for your body and your life, changes everything.

My approach is direct and individualized. Whether you've never trained consistently or you've been at it for years without seeing results, I'll build a program that actually works — and stay present to make sure it does.

Every client is treated as an individual.

I coach multiple clients simultaneously, but each person receives focused attention, real responses, and a plan built specifically around them — not a template with their name on it.

Accountability is built into the structure.

You choose how often we check in — from once a week to every single day. The frequency adapts to your schedule and what you need to stay on track.

Results require a sustainable approach.

I don't sell shortcuts. I build programs that integrate into your actual life, so the changes you make hold well beyond our time working together.

What I Offer

Services Designed Around Your Goals

From full monthly coaching to one-time plans — clear options for every starting point and level of commitment.

01

Fat Loss Coaching

Targeted programming and nutrition strategy built for sustainable fat loss. Calorie targets, macronutrient ratios, and training structured to produce consistent, measurable results over time.

02

Body Recomposition

Losing fat and building muscle simultaneously requires precision. This is a specialty — I'll design the exact approach your physique needs and adjust the program as your body responds and adapts.

03

Nutrition Planning

Practical, personalized dietary guidance you can maintain long-term. No elimination protocols or crash diets — a structured approach to eating that genuinely supports your training and daily life.

04

Custom Workout Routines

A fully individualized training program built around your available equipment, schedule, and current fitness level. Ideal for those who want expert structure without ongoing monthly coaching.

05

Ongoing Accountability

Regular check-ins on your schedule — once a week or daily, your choice. Progress reviews, real-time plan adjustments, and consistent communication from a coach invested in your consistency.

06

Beginner Foundations

No prior gym experience required. I'll walk you through how to train effectively, how to eat with purpose, and how to build the habits that produce results — without confusion or overwhelm.
